Beforehand Notes:

I would just like to recommend that you use a different web browser than the one that is provided to you (i.e. Google Chrome or Edge). I would recommend you use an alternative one that you can install your own extensions on. These are the one I would recommend:

  • Orion (Privacy respective with UI similar to Safari)
  • Brave
  • Vivaldi
  • Arc (New kid on the block with and interesting, look but has a learning curve)
  • Zen (Newer kid on the block with a similar style to Arc)
  • Chromium
  • Opera (Not trustworthy ownership, likely to steal and sell data)
  • If it would work, I would highly recommend a Firefox fork. (i.e. Firefox, Librewolf, Waterfox, and Floorp)

The extensions that I recommend you install on whatever browser you have chosen are:

  • Ublock Origin (Could soon not work due to Manifest V3 rollout, alternatively use Ublock Origin Lite or Adguard Experimental)
  • (Not necessary) Decentraleyes (Could also be affected by MV3 rollout)
  • (Not necessary) Privacy Badger
  • (Not necessary) Fastforward
